## Overview

SPYLT is a high-energy beverage site centered on protein + caffeine drinks.
The page combines oversized typography, loud flavor storytelling, and
conversion-focused sections so the brand feels playful, memorable, and easy to
scan.

## What stands out

- The hero opens with a clear protein + caffeine message and an immediate call
  to action.
- Flavor discovery is front and center, with a strong product lineup that makes
  the brand feel broad but focused.
- The benefits section turns shelf stability, lactose-free positioning, and
  recyclability into quick trust signals.
- Social proof, newsletter capture, and responsible-consumption messaging round
  out the page with credibility and conversion cues.

## Page structure

- Hero section with the SPYLT positioning and product-first visual treatment.
- Flavor showcase for Chocolate Milk, Strawberry Milk, Cookies & Cream,
  Peanut Butter Chocolate, Vanilla Milkshake, and Max Chocolate Milk.
- Benefits block highlighting the practical reasons to choose the product.
- Community and conversation section that reinforces brand buzz.
- Footer area with site navigation, newsletter signup, and legal links.
