## Overview

Nimbus is a premium mechanical keyboard site built around customization,
performance, and modern industrial design. The page combines sleek product
visuals, enthusiast-focused feature highlights, and conversion-oriented
sections so the keyboard feels both premium and approachable.

## What stands out

- The hero opens with a striking keyboard showcase and a clear premium-product
  message.
- Customization is front and center, allowing visitors to explore layouts,
  switches, and personalization options.
- Feature sections highlight build quality, responsiveness, and the typing
  experience through clean visual storytelling.
- Product-focused calls to action, supporting specifications, and conversion
  sections make the experience feel polished and commerce-ready.

## Page structure

- Hero section with the Nimbus positioning and product-first visual treatment.
- Feature showcase highlighting premium materials, build quality, and typing
  performance.
- Customization section focused on switches, layouts, and personalization.
- Product details block reinforcing technical specifications and key benefits.
- Conversion-focused call-to-action sections encouraging exploration and
  purchase.
- Footer area with site navigation, product links, and supporting resources.
