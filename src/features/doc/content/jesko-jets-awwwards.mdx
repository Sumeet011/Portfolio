## Overview

Jesko Jets is a premium aviation website concept focused on clarity, speed, and a refined visual system. I designed the UI/UX to highlight luxury services, quick inquiry paths, and cinematic imagery without overwhelming the user.

## What I focused on

- Editorial layouts that balance large visuals with short, scannable copy.
- Clear hierarchy for service tiers, fleet highlights, and CTA blocks.
- Smooth section transitions and restrained motion to keep the experience calm.
- High-contrast typography and generous spacing for a premium feel.

## Notes

This is a UI/UX portfolio project. The goal was to craft a clean, award-ready presentation for a private aviation brand with a focus on elegance and usability.
