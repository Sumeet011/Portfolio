## Overview

Phone Wraps is a phone covers and accessories brand startup I worked with as a full-stack engineer. The goal was to build a clean, premium storefront that keeps the focus on the products while making discovery and purchase feel effortless.

## My role

As a full-stack engineer, I worked across the UI and the data layer to deliver a polished, production-ready shopping experience.

## What I focused on

- Strong product presentation with clear pricing and CTA placement.
- Simple navigation for fast category and model discovery.
- Clean typography and spacing to keep the catalog readable.
- Visual hierarchy that supports conversion without feeling crowded.
